About Doctor

Professional Medical Care with Cardiology Expertise

Dr. Md. Abdul Hamid Sagar is a cardiology and medicine specialist with postgraduate qualifications in Medicine and Cardiology. He has served in several respected hospitals in Bangladesh, including Chattogram Medical College Hospital, Rangamati Medical College Hospital, and National Institute of Cardiovascular Diseases.

His clinical interests include heart disease, ECG evaluation, hypertension, chest disease, angiogram follow-up, stent/ring follow-up, pacemaker consultation, and general medicine.
